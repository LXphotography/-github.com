💡 Essential Studio Enhancements

Proofing Galleries: Never send unedited photos via email. Integrate free tools like Pixieset or PassGallery to allow clients to favorite and select photos securely.

Reminders: Setting up SMS automation to go out 24 hours before a shoot drops client no-show rates by nearly 85%.
